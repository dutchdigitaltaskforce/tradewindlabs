Principles

Why we build this way

Every design decision at TradeWind Labs starts from the same question: does this protect the user?

🔒

Privacy by default

No PII collected from regular users. Data minimization is a requirement, not a feature flag.

⚓

Self-custody first

We never hold your funds. Wallets stay in your hands. Smart-contract escrow, not platform escrow.

🔗

Open infrastructure

Public APIs, verifiable on-chain data, and no lock-in. Build on top of what we build.

⚡

Ship, then improve

We prefer working software in users' hands over perfect software on a whiteboard.
